# 12 Real World Case Studies

## Scenario 1: Network Outage due to ISP Failure  
### Design Challenge:  
ISP failure led to a complete network outage affecting business operations.  
### Solution Implemented:  
A backup ISP was activated with a redundant network setup.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Always have a backup ISP and regularly test failover.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Relying only on a single ISP without redundancy.

---

## Scenario 2: Security Breach via Phishing  
### Design Challenge:  
Employees fell victim to phishing attacks, compromising internal systems.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Security awareness training and enhanced email filtering.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Continuous training in cybersecurity is essential.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Ignoring the human factor in security protocols.

---

## Scenario 3: Slow Network Performance  
### Design Challenge:  
Users experienced sluggish network speeds during peak hours.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Bandwidth management and Quality of Service (QoS) configurations.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Monitor network usage actively and manage bandwidth accordingly.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Neglecting to analyze traffic patterns.

---

## Scenario 4: Misconfigured Router  
### Design Challenge:  
Network devices were not communicating due to router misconfigurations.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Router configurations were reviewed and corrected.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Regularly verify router settings and documentation.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Outdated network documentation leading to errors.

---

## Scenario 5: IoT Device Vulnerability  
### Design Challenge:  
Unsecured IoT devices became entry points for cyber attacks.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Isolated IoT devices on a separate VLAN.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Segregate IoT devices to enhance security.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Failing to secure IoT devices at the network layer.

---

## Scenario 6: Cloud Service Outage  
### Design Challenge:  
Business operations halted due to cloud provider downtime.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Local backups and hybrid solutions were established.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Diversify cloud service providers and maintain local backups.  
### Common Mistakes:  
Over-dependence on a single cloud provider.

### Lessons Learned:

## Scenario 10: Wi-Fi Coverage Gaps  
### Design Challenge:  
Areas within the office had poor Wi-Fi connectivity.  
### Solution Implemented:  
Conducted a site survey and added additional access points.  
### Lessons Learned:  
Regularly assess and improve Wi-Fi coverage.  
### Common Mistakes:
